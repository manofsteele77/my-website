About the studio

A small studio, built for Grande Prairie and the Peace Country.

Aurora Digital is a one-person design studio focused on two things: rebuilding websites that have fallen behind, and producing AI-generated video that makes local businesses look current.

Most small businesses in Grande Prairie are working with sites that were built years ago — slow, hard to update on a phone, and not built for how people actually search and shop today. Aurora Digital exists to fix that, without the overhead of a big agency.

Every project starts with a real conversation about your business, your customers, and what's actually getting in the way online. From there, I design and build a site (or a video) that's tailored to that — not a recycled template with your logo dropped in.

The name comes from somewhere familiar to anyone who's spent a winter night here: the aurora. Bands of green and violet light moving across a dark northern sky — distinctive, a little unexpected, and unmistakably ours. That's the feeling the studio's work is built around.

How we work

Three things every project gets.

No matter the size of the project, these stay constant.

CLEAN CODE

Built to last

No bloated builders or plugin pile-ups. Sites are built with clean, fast code that's easy to maintain — and easy to hand off if you ever need someone else to take over.

REAL DESIGN

Made for your brand
